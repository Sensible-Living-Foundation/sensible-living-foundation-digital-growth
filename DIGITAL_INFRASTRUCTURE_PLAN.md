# Sensible Living Foundation Digital Infrastructure Plan

## Purpose

This document outlines the digital systems that support the Sensible Living Foundation’s mission and operations.

The goal is to create a simple, scalable digital ecosystem that helps the organization:

- reach more people
- attract donors
- recruit volunteers
- build partnerships
- communicate impact

This plan guides contributors who are helping improve the foundation’s digital infrastructure.

---

# Core Digital Systems

The foundation’s digital presence consists of several connected systems.

## Website

The website is the central hub for:

- explaining the mission
- presenting programs
- accepting donations
- recruiting volunteers
- connecting with partners

Current website:

https://www.sensiblelivingfoundation.org

Future improvements may include:

- clearer homepage messaging
- improved program pages
- improved donation flow
- stronger calls to action
- improved SEO

---

## Donation System

Donations are currently processed through Givebutter.

Goals:

- encourage recurring donors
- communicate clearly how funds are used
- send automatic thank-you messages
- follow up with impact updates

Future improvements:

- donor journey mapping
- improved donation page design
- donor communication automation

---

## Email Communication

Email is one of the most important ways to stay connected with supporters.

Email should be used for:

- newsletters
- program updates
- donor communication
- volunteer communication
- partnership outreach

Future improvements:

- automated welcome emails
- automated donor follow-up
- monthly newsletters
- segmented email lists

---

## Volunteer Management

The foundation should have a clear system for recruiting and engaging volunteers.

Volunteer forms should capture:

- name
- email
- phone number
- skills
- interests
- availability

Volunteer categories may include:

- Sense Gardens
- financial literacy programs
- community events
- marketing and outreach
- operations support

---

## Corporate Partnerships

Businesses and organizations should be able to easily partner with the foundation.

The website should support:

- corporate sponsorship opportunities
- employee volunteer programs
- partnership inquiries
- collaboration opportunities

---

## Analytics and Measurement

The foundation should track website performance.

Recommended tools:

- Google Analytics
- Google Search Console

Key metrics to track:

- website traffic
- donation activity
- newsletter signups
- volunteer inquiries
- partnership inquiries

---

# Long-Term Opportunities

Future digital improvements may include:

- donor relationship management system (CRM)
- volunteer management system
- online education programs
- digital storytelling and impact reporting

---

# Contributor Role

Contributors should focus on improvements that are:

- affordable for nonprofits
- easy to maintain
- aligned with the foundation mission
- practical to implement

Recommendations should prioritize simplicity and sustainability.
